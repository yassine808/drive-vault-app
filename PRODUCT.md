# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Security-conscious individuals who want a local-first encrypted vault for passwords, private notes, job applications, and TOTP codes. They value privacy, craft, and tools that feel considered rather than mass-produced. They use Vault daily as a trusted companion — not a utility they forget about.

## Product Purpose

Vault is a desktop encrypted vault app (Electron + Supabase) that stores passwords, notes, job applications, and TOTP secrets. All sensitive data is AES-encrypted client-side. The app should feel like a premium personal tool — quiet confidence, zero clutter, every pixel intentional.

## Brand Personality

**Refined, precise, warm.** Like a well-made leather wallet that ages beautifully. Not a corporate tool. Not a startup plaything. Something you trust and enjoy opening.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS dashboards (gradient heroes, identical card grids, big metric templates)
- Typical fintech (navy-and-gold, stock-photo energy)
- Over-designed Electron apps (glassmorphism everywhere, excessive purple)
- Anything that looks like it was generated from a template

## Design Principles

1. **Quiet confidence** — The UI should feel assured, not loud. Restraint over decoration.
2. **Editorial craft** — Typography and spacing should feel like a well-designed document, not a dashboard.
3. **Warm precision** — Warm tones, but exacting alignment. Human, not cold.
4. **Progressive disclosure** — Show only what's needed. Reveal depth on demand.
5. **No AI slop** — If someone could look at this and say "AI made that," it has failed.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA minimum for text contrast
- Reduced motion support via `prefers-reduced-motion`
- Keyboard-navigable throughout
- No reliance on color alone for status communication
